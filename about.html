About Me

Changqian Yu

Senior Technical Specialist @ Kunlun Tech

As a Senior Technical Specialist at Kunlun Tech, I lead a team developing cutting-edge large multimodal models, with expertise in Diffusion and VLM technologies. My tenure at Meituan’s Autonomous Delivery Department equipped me with a robust understanding of real-world AI applications.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2021 from the Huazhong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Nong Sang and Prof. Changxin Gao. I also studied at the University of Adelaide, working with Prof. Chunhua Shen, and received the Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Award.

I also interned at Microsoft Research Asia and Megvii (Face++), collaborating with Dr. Jingdong Wang, Dr. Gang Yu, and Dr. Jian Sun.
